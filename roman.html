The history of rome spans over 12 centuries,from the founding of rome in 753BC to the fall of the western roman empire in 476AD.Rome was founded by romulus and remus, two legendary brothers who were said to have been raised by a she-wolf.Romulus bacame the first king of rome.

Roman kingdom(753BC-509BC)

The roman kingdom was ruled by seven kings,including romulus.During this period, rome developed it's system of government laws and architecture.

Roman repubic

The roman republic was estabished after the expulsion of the last king, lucius tarquinius superbus.During this period,rome expanded it's territories through a series of conquest,including the punic wars against carthage.

Roman empire(27BC-476AD)

The roamn empire was established by august caesar,who became the first emperor of rome. During this period,rome reached it's peak of power and prosperity,with a vast network of roads,aqueducts and public buildings.

Fall of roman empire(476AD)

The western roman empire was eventually overrun by the barbarian tribe,including the visigoths and the vandal. The last roman emperor,Romulus augustulus,was deposed in 476AD
