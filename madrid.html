Madrid city

Founded

The capital city of spain,is such a rich and fascinating one spanning over 900 years. It was founded in the 9th century by the moors,who built a fortress on the site of the present-day royal place.The city was known as "migerit" or "mayrit".After the christian reconquest of spain,madrid became small but strategic town.

Habsburg rule(16th-17th centuries)

In 1561,king philip ii of spain moved the royal court to madrid making it the capital of spain.During this period madrid experienced significant growth and development,with the construction of grand building including the royal palace and plaza mayor.

Enlightenment and reform(18th century)

The 18th century saw significant reforms and modernization in madrid,with the construction of new roads,bridges and public buildings.The city became a centre of culture and learning, with the establishment of royal academy of fine arts and national library.

War and turmoil (19th-20th centuries)

Madrid sufferd significantly during the spanish war of independence against napoleon and the spanish civil war.The city was also affected by the economic(crisis of 20th century)

Modern madrid(late 20th century-present)

In recent decades,madrid has undergone significant transformation,with major urban development projects,including the expansion of metro system and creation of new public spaces.Today, madrid is a thriving,cosmopitan cityknown for it's vibrant cultural scene,rich history, and warm hospitality.some popular historical landmarks in madrid include;royal palace of madrid, plaza mayor,puerta del sol,retiro park,prado museum.
